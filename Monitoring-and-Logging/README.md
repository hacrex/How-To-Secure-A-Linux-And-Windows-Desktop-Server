## Introduction

Proactive monitoring and comprehensive logging are the eyes and ears of your security infrastructure. They provide the visibility needed to identify anomalies, detect attacks in progress, and gather forensic evidence after an incident. This section delves into the tools and techniques that enable robust security oversight, alongside specific guidance on common threats and client-side security measures.
